About

A home for founders
who refuse to settle.

Welcome to Founders House — the hub for Stockholm's most promising AI startups. We support the next generation of unicorn founders through a tight-knit community built on high ambition, collaboration, and shared energy — all under the same roof.

When we're not building the future, we host inspiring events with top entrepreneurs and ecosystem leaders, casual afterworks, and provide valuable resources to help our members thrive.

Founders first. No strings attached.
